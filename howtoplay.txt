                       HOW   TO   PLAY
1) Kill the creeps within the limited time to win.
2) Move Bomberman with the arrow keys.
3) Set a bomb with the space bar. Bombs will explode after a certain amount of time.
4) Clear a path for yourself by destroying the red boxes with your bombs.

                         Warnings
1) Bombs are lethal to most creatures and should be used with caution.
2) Creeps are so strong that they touch Bomberman and he dies.
3) Gray boxes are the strongest entities in this universe and cannot be destroyed by anything. Avoid picking a fight with gray boxes.
